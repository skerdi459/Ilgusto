Our Story

A family table, set with the day's finest catch

For over twenty-five years, Il Gusto has welcomed guests to a place where the sea meets the Italian kitchen. Every morning our counter fills with whole fish, live lobster, langoustines and red prawns on ice — chosen by hand, prepared simply, and served the way it should be.

From silky lobster linguine and ravioli rolled fresh each day to dry-aged steaks from the grill, our cooking honours tradition without fuss. Pair it with a bottle from a cellar that runs from Chianti Classico to Dom Pérignon, and let the evening unfold.

Come hungry. Leave like family.
